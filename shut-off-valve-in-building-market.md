<p><p>Shut-off valves are crucial components in building systems, controlling fluid flow for maintenance and safety. The ball valve features a spherical disc that offers minimal resistance and swift operation. Butterfly valves use a rotating disc to regulate flow, ideal for large volumes. Gate valves provide on/off control with a wedge-shaped gate, suitable for full flow applications. Globe valves, with their spherical body, are designed for throttling control, making them effective for regulating flow in various systems. Each type serves specific operational needs.</p></p>
