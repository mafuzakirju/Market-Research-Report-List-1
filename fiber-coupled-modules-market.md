<p><strong>The Fiber Coupled Modules Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitter-Based FCM</li><li>Bar-Based FCM</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Coupled Modules (FCM) market is primarily categorized into two types: Emitter-Based FCM and Bar-Based FCM. Emitter-Based FCMs utilize single, discrete light emitters, offering high precision and flexibility for various applications. In contrast, Bar-Based FCMs consist of multiple emitters arranged in a bar, providing higher power output and efficiency for applications requiring larger light intensities. Both types serve essential roles in industries like telecommunications, medical, and industrial automation, facilitating effective light coupling for diverse functionalities.</p></p>
