<p><p>Time of Flight Secondary Ion Mass Spectrometry (TOF-SIMS) is an advanced analytical technique that provides detailed information about the composition of surfaces and thin films at a molecular level. It works by bombarding a material with a focused primary ion beam, resulting in the ejection of secondary ions that are then analyzed based on their mass-to-charge ratio. TOF-SIMS is widely utilized in various industries, including materials science, semiconductor manufacturing, and biomedicine, due to its high spatial resolution and sensitivity to surface and near-surface compositions.</p><p>The TOF-SIMS market is poised for significant growth, driven by the increasing demand for advanced surface characterization techniques in research and industrial applications.
