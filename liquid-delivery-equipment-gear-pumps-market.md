<p><strong>The Liquid Delivery Equipment Gear Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Delivery Equipment Gear Pumps market consists of two main types: external gear pumps and internal gear pumps. External gear pumps utilize two interlocking gears rotating in opposite directions to transfer fluid, ideal for high-viscosity liquids. Internal gear pumps feature a rotor and an outer gear, providing excellent suction capabilities and a smooth, pulsation-free flow, suitable for various applications. Both types are essential for precise liquid handling in industries such as oil and gas, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
