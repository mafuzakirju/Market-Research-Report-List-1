<p><p>The sleeve labels market encompasses two primary types: stretch sleeves and shrink sleeves. Stretch sleeves are made from elastic materials that conform tightly to the shape of the container, providing a vibrant and seamless design. Conversely, shrink sleeves utilize heat to shrink around the package, ensuring a snug fit and a 360-degree print area. Both types offer unique advantages, such as high visibility, durability, and the ability to accommodate complex shapes, making them popular choices for branding and packaging applications.</p></p>
