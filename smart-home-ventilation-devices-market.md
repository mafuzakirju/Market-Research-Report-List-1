<p><p>Smart home ventilation devices enhance indoor air quality through various mechanisms. Exhaust ventilation devices efficiently remove stale air, pollutants, and excess moisture, ensuring a healthy living environment. Supply ventilation devices introduce fresh outdoor air, balancing indoor air pressure and improving comfort. Other devices may include heat recovery ventilators and energy recovery ventilators, which transfer heat or energy between incoming and outgoing air, optimizing energy use. Together, these devices contribute to a smarter, healthier, and more energy-efficient home.</p></p>
