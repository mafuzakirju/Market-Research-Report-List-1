<p><p>The micro pipette tips market consists of two primary types: non-filtered and filtered pipette tips. Non-filtered tips are designed for general liquid transfer, offering precision and accuracy without additional barrier features. Conversely, filtered pipette tips incorporate a hydrophobic filter, preventing contamination from aerosols and liquid splashes, thereby enhancing sample integrity. These tips cater to various laboratory applications, with the choice between them depending on the required level of protection and precision for sensitive experiments.</p></p>
