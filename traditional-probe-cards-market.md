<p><strong>The Traditional Probe Cards Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blade/Tungsten Probe Cards</li><li>Epoxy/Cantilever Probe Cards</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The traditional probe cards market is primarily segmented into two types: blade/tungsten probe cards and epoxy/cantilever probe cards. Blade/tungsten probe cards utilize sharp tungsten pins for high-precision testing and durability, ideal for high-density applications. In contrast, epoxy/cantilever probe cards feature flexible, epoxy-based materials that allow for better alignment and lower contact resistance, making them suitable for a range of semiconductor tests. Both types cater to different testing requirements in semiconductor manufacturing and development processes.</p></p>
