<p><p>The Apparel Reflective Tape market is primarily divided into two key types: glass beads materials and microprismatic materials. Glass beads materials utilize tiny beads that reflect light, providing visibility in low-light conditions. This type is often cost-effective and commonly used in workwear and safety apparel. In contrast, microprismatic materials feature advanced technology with micro-sized prisms that enhance light reflection, offering superior visibility. These are typically used in high-performance applications where safety is critical, such as emergency services and industrial workwear.</p></p>
