<p><p>The golf rangefinder market is primarily divided into two types: laser and GPS rangefinders. Laser golf rangefinders use laser technology to accurately measure distances to specific targets on the course, offering precision and immediate feedback. They are favored for their accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, GPS golf rangefinders utilize satellite signals to provide distance information based on course maps. They often include additional features like course layouts and statistics, making them popular for golfers seeking an all-in-one device.</p></p>
