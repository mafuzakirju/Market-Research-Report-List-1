<p><p>The sport touchscreen gloves market is primarily divided into two types: resistive and capacitive touchscreen gloves. Resistive touchscreen gloves are designed to work by applying pressure, making them compatible with various touchscreen devices, but they may lack precision. Capacitive touchscreen gloves, on the other hand, utilize conductive materials that allow for more accurate and responsive interaction with modern touchscreens. As users seek functionality and comfort in different weather conditions, these glove types cater to distinct preferences and usage scenarios in outdoor sports.</p></p>
