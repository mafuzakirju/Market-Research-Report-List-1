<p><p>A passive desiccant wheel is an innovative technology used in HVAC systems to control humidity levels without consuming significant energy. Comprising materials that adsorb moisture from the air, these wheels rotate between incoming and outgoing airstreams, facilitating dehydration of the incoming air while regenerating through airflow from the outgoing stream. This type of system is environmentally friendly and energy efficient compared to traditional dehumidification methods.</p><p>The Passive Desiccant Wheel Market is experiencing robust growth, driven by increasing demand for energy-efficient climate control solutions in residential, commercial, and industrial applications.
