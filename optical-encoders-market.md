<p><strong>The Optical Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Encoders</li><li>Absolute Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical encoders market is primarily divided into two types: incremental and absolute encoders. Incremental encoders generate a series of pulses that indicate changes in position, measuring the movement relative to a reference point. They are often used in applications requiring precise motion control. In contrast, absolute encoders provide unique position values for each shaft angle, maintaining accurate location even after power loss. Both types find applications in various industries, including robotics, automotive, and industrial automation, catering to diverse positioning needs.</p></p>
