<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Clinical Electrophoresis Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Slab Electrophoresis</li><li>Zone Electrophoresis</li><li>Paper Electrophoresis</li><li>Gel Electrophoresis</li><li>Isoelectric-Focusing</li><li>Immune-Electrophoresis</li><li>Capillary Electrophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The clinical electrophoresis market is segmented into various types based on their methodologies. Slab electrophoresis involves separating molecules in flat gels, while zone electrophoresis uses different mediums for separation based on charge. Paper electrophoresis employs cellulose paper as a medium, while gel electrophoresis utilizes gel matrices for enhanced resolution. Isoelectric focusing separates proteins based on their isoelectric points. Immune-electrophoresis combines electrophoresis with immunological techniques, and capillary electrophoresis offers high-resolution separation in narrow capillaries, improving analysis speed and efficiency.</p></p>
