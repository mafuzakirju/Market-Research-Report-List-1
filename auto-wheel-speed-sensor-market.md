<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Auto Wheel Speed Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto wheel speed sensor market is primarily divided into Hall Effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize magnetic fields to detect wheel speed, providing precise feedback for traction and stability control systems. They are widely used for their durability and accuracy. In contrast, magnetic electric sensors operate through an electromagnetic induction principle, generating voltage in response to wheel rotation. Both types are critical for vehicle safety and performance, enabling advanced driver assistance systems and anti-lock braking systems.</p></p>
