<p><p>The thermoplastic unidirectional tape market includes various types, each with distinct properties. Polyamide (PA) is valued for its durability and flexibility, suitable for a range of industrial uses. Polypropylene (PP) provides lightweight and cost-effective options, often used in consumer goods. Polyphenylene Sulfide (PPS) demonstrates excellent chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it suitable for demanding environments, including electrical and automotive sectors.</p></p>
