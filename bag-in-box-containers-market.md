<p><p>Bag-in-Box containers are flexible packaging solutions used for liquids, comprised of an inner bag and an outer box. The LDPE type is made from low-density polyethylene, offering good moisture resistance and cost-effectiveness. The EVA type, made with ethylene-vinyl acetate, provides enhanced clarity and durability, making it suitable for products requiring preservation. The EVOH type, with ethylene-vinyl alcohol, excels in barrier properties against oxygen and is ideal for sensitive products, ensuring extended shelf life. Each type serves distinct industry needs.</p></p>
