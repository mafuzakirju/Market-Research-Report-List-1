<p><strong>The Automotive High-Pressure Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger High-Pressure Pump</li><li>Port and Helix High-Pressure Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive high-pressure pump market comprises two main types: plunger high-pressure pumps and port and helix high-pressure pumps. Plunger high-pressure pumps utilize a reciprocating mechanism to generate high pressure, making them suitable for fuel injection systems in internal combustion engines. In contrast, port and helix high-pressure pumps employ a helical design to enhance flow efficiency and pressure stability, commonly used in diesel fuel systems. Both types are essential for optimizing vehicle performance and meeting stringent emissions standards.</p></p>
