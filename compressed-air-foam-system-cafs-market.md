<p><strong>The Compressed Air Foam System (CAFS) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low-expansion Foam System</li><li>Medium-expansion Foam System</li><li>High-expansion Foam System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Compressed Air Foam Systems (CAFS) utilize a mixture of air, water, and foam concentrate to create a versatile firefighting medium. The market is categorized into three types based on foam expansion. Low-expansion foam systems produce a dense foam suitable for suppressing flammable liquid fires. Medium-expansion systems offer broader coverage for various fire types, including solid combustibles. High-expansion foam systems generate large volumes of foam, effectively filling enclosed spaces to extinguish fires by displacing oxygen, making them ideal for warehouse and storage fires.</p></p>
