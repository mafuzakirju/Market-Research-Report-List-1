<p><strong>The High Accuracy Rotary Encoder Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Incremental Rotary Encoders</li><li>Absolute Rotary Encoders</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high accuracy rotary encoder market is primarily divided into two types: incremental and absolute rotary encoders. Incremental rotary encoders provide information on the position of a shaft relative to a starting point, generating pulses that count rotations. In contrast, absolute rotary encoders deliver a unique positional value for each shaft position, allowing for precise tracking without a reference point. Both types are essential in automation, robotics, and industrial applications, ensuring accurate movement and control in various systems.</p></p>
