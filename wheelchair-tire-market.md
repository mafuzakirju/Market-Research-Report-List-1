<p><p>The wheelchair tire market features several types tailored to different terrains and usage. **Slick tires** offer smooth surfaces for indoor or pavement use, ensuring minimal rolling resistance. **Semi-slick tires** strike a balance, providing light tread for some outdoor grip while maintaining speed. **Inverted tread tires** feature unique patterns for enhanced traction on slippery surfaces. Lastly, **knobby tires** are designed for off-road conditions, providing maximum grip on rough terrains. Each type serves specific needs for wheelchair users, enhancing mobility and comfort.</p></p>
