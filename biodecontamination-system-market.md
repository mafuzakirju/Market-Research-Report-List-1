<p><p>The biodecontamination system market primarily consists of two types: chamber decontamination and room decontamination. Chamber decontamination systems are designed for isolated environments, typically employed for sterilizing equipment and materials within enclosed spaces. In contrast, room decontamination systems target larger areas, effectively sanitizing entire rooms or facilities, often using methods like vaporized hydrogen peroxide or UV light. Both systems play crucial roles in ensuring safety and reducing contamination risks in healthcare, pharmaceutical, and laboratory settings.</p></p>
