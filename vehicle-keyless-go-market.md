<p><strong>The Vehicle Keyless Go Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Keyless Access (PKES)</li><li>Remote Keyless Access (RKES)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Keyless Go market comprises two main types: Passive Keyless Access (PKES) and Remote Keyless Access (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicles without physically interacting with a key fob, relying on proximity sensors to detect the key's presence. In contrast, RKES involves a remote key fob that allows users to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle from a distance. Both systems enhance convenience and security, catering to evolving consumer preferences for advanced automotive technologies.</p></p>
