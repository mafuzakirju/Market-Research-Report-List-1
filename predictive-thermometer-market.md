<p><p>The Predictive Thermometer market is divided into two main types: contact type and non-contact type thermometers. Contact type thermometers measure temperature by physically touching the skin, providing accurate readings but requiring close interaction. In contrast, non-contact type thermometers use infrared technology to measure temperature from a distance, allowing for quick and hygienic assessments without direct contact. Both types serve crucial roles in medical settings, personal health monitoring, and industries where temperature regulation is vital.</p></p>
