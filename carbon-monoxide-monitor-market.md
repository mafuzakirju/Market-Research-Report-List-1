<p><p>The carbon monoxide monitor market is divided into two primary types: fixed and portable monitors. Fixed monitors are permanently installed in specific locations, typically in homes, commercial buildings, or industrial sites, providing continuous air quality monitoring and early detection of carbon monoxide leaks. In contrast, portable monitors are handheld devices that can be easily moved, allowing for flexible testing in various environments, such as vehicles or personal spaces. Both types are essential for ensuring safety and preventing CO-related health hazards.</p></p>
