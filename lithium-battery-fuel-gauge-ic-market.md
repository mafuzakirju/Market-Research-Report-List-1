<p><p>The lithium battery fuel gauge IC market is divided into two main types: single-cell and multi-cell. Single-cell fuel gauge ICs are designed for individual lithium-ion cells, providing precise state-of-charge information for compact devices like wearables and smartphones. In contrast, multi-cell fuel gauge ICs monitor multiple battery cells in parallel or series configurations, commonly used in electric vehicles and energy storage systems. Each type serves different applications, balancing complexity and efficiency based on power requirements and device specifications.</p></p>
