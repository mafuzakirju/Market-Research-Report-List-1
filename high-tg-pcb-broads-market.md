<p><p>High-Tg PCB boards are classified based on their glass transition temperature (Tg). The Normal market includes boards with Tg values of 130℃, 135℃, and 140℃, ideal for standard applications with moderate thermal demands. The Medium market features Tg levels at 150℃ and 155℃, suitable for more demanding environments, such as automotive and industrial. The High market encompasses boards with Tg above 170℃, designed for extreme conditions, like aerospace and high-frequency applications, ensuring durability and reliability under significant thermal stress.</p></p>
