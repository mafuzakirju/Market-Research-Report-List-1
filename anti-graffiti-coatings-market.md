<p><p>The anti-graffiti coatings market features three primary types: </p><p>1. **Sacrificial Coatings** are affordable and easily removed, designed to absorb graffiti and require frequent reapplication after cleaning. </p><p>2. **Semi-sacrificial Coatings** offer a balance between longevity and ease of maintenance, allowing for multiple cleanings before reapplication is necessary. </p><p>3. **Permanent Coatings** provide durable protection and are designed to withstand multiple graffiti attacks, making them ideal for high-traffic areas, although they may be more expensive initially. Each type serves different needs and budgets for graffiti prevention.</p></p>
