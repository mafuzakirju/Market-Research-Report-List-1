<p><strong>The Proximity Sensor for Smartphone Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical Displacement Sensor</li><li>Ultrasonic Displacement Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Proximity sensors in smartphones primarily include optical displacement sensors and ultrasonic displacement sensors. Optical sensors use infrared light to detect nearby objects, enabling functions like screen dimming during calls. They are renowned for their accuracy and low power consumption. In contrast, ultrasonic sensors emit sound waves that bounce back from objects, allowing the device to determine proximity through time-of-flight measurements. This technology offers effective detection at varying distances, often enhancing features like gesture recognition and user interaction.</p></p>
