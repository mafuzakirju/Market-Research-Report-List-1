<p><p>The plasma expander market includes several types of products used to increase blood volume in clinical settings. Dextran is a polysaccharide that helps maintain blood pressure, while Hydroxyethyl Starch is a synthetic polymer that improves hemodynamic stability. Human Albumin, derived from human plasma, is essential for treating hypoproteinemia. PEGylated Albumin enhances stability and circulation time. Polyvinylpyrrolidone (PVP) offers a synthetic alternative to natural expanders, and Gelatin, derived from collagen, serves as a biocompatible option for volume replacement.</p></p>
