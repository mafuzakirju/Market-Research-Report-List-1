<p><p>The plain bearing market is segmented into various types based on their operational functions. Journal bearings facilitate rotary motion, supporting shafts in engines and turbines. Linear bearings enable smooth sliding motion, ideal for applications like conveyor systems. Thrust bearings manage axial loads, commonly found in automotive and aerospace industries. Angular contact bearings handle combined radial and axial loads, often used in precision machinery. Each type caters to specific needs in various industrial applications, contributing to diverse market dynamics.</p></p>
