The market is segmented into two main types: positive-pressure SARs, which maintain a higher air pressure inside the mask to prevent contaminants from entering, ensuring a secure fit; and negative-pressure SARs, which rely on the user to create a seal and draw external air through filters, suitable for less hazardous environments. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications and safety standards.</p></p>
