<p><p>Optical networking and communication encompass various market segments essential for data transmission over fiber optics. Transceivers facilitate the conversion of electrical signals to optical signals and vice versa, enabling seamless communication. Amplifiers boost signal strength over long distances, ensuring clarity and minimal loss. Switches manage the routing of optical signals between different network paths. Splitters distribute signals to multiple outputs, enhancing connectivity. Circulators redirect light between ports, improving signal management and efficiency within the network infrastructure.</p></p>
